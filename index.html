Stop Touching Your Face

This application will track your face and hands and warn you whenever you are touching your face.


Camera feed goes here

Why?

Public Health Canada recommends the following to avoid virus propagation. One of the recommendations is to avoid touching your face. I never realized how much I touch my face until I saw all those recommendations from doctors on how to avoid getting the Covid-19 virus. So I built an app to remind me to stop touching my face. To help stop the virus propagation, you can do those simple things.

Wash Your Hands

wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after using the washroom and when preparing food use alcohol-based hand sanitizer if soap and water are not available

Coughing and Sneezing

When coughing or sneezing: cough or sneeze into a tissue or the bend of your arm, not your hand dispose of any tissues you have used as soon as possible in a lined waste basket and wash your hands afterwards

Clean Surface

Clean the following high-touch surfaces frequently with regular household cleaners or diluted bleach (1 part bleach to 9 parts water): toys, toilets, phones, electronics, door handles, bedside tables, television remotes.
